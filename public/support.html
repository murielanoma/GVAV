Our Key Programs

Your contribution will directly support the following initiatives:

  • Helping Needy Students: We provide scholarships and resources to underprivileged students, ensuring they have equal access to education.
  • Creating Job Opportunities: Training and mentorship programs empower young individuals, widows, and others to gain valuable skills for employment or entrepreneurship.
  • Spoken English Training: We help individuals of all ages develop their spoken English skills through programs in Thaneeruttu and Mulliyawalai.
  • Computer Literacy Classes: Our computer lab, equipped with 15 computers, offers training to students and jobseekers, bridging the digital divide.
  • Daycare Services: We provide daycare support, enabling mothers to pursue employment or education opportunities while their children are cared for in a safe environment.
  • Sewing Classes for Women: Our sewing program empowers women to gain skills that allow them to start businesses and become financially independent.
  • Career Guidance: We provide career counseling sessions in collaboration with experienced facilitators to help individuals make informed decisions about their future.

How Your Contribution Helps

We offer all our programs free of charge, but we rely on donations to cover essential expenses such as teacher salaries, rent, and electricity. Your contribution ensures the continuation of these programs and allows us to expand our reach.

You can choose to support a specific individual or contribute to the Trust's general fund. Please mention your preference when making a donation.
